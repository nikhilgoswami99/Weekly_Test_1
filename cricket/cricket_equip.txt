Cricket Equipment :-
Racket/Bat: A long, flat bat made of wood or other materials.
Ball: A hard, leather-covered ball with a cork core.
Net: A sight screen or net to help batsmen see the ball.
Protective Gear: Helmet, leg pads, thigh guards, arm guards, chest guards, elbow guards, gloves, jockstrap, abdominal guard.
Shoes: Spiked shoes for grip and stability.
